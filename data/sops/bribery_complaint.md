# SOP: Filing a Complaint for Bribery (India)

If a public servant asks for a bribe, do NOT pay it. Follow these steps:

1. **Record Evidence**: Use your phone to record the conversation or a spy camera if possible. Note the time, date, and name of the official.
2. **Contact the Anti-Corruption Bureau (ACB)**: Every state has an ACB. You can also contact the Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI) for central government officials.
    - **ACB Helpline**: 1064 (National Anti-Corruption Helpline)
3. **File a Written Complaint**: Submit a formal complaint along with the evidence.
4. **Witnesses**: If there are people around, try to get their contact details as witnesses.

**Red Flags**:
- Do not pay the bribe even if they threaten to delay your work.
- Do not attempt to trap the official yourself; leave it to the ACB/CBI.
- It is illegal for a public servant to demand an 'extra fee' for a free service.
