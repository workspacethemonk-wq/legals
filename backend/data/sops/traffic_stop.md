# SOP: Traffic Stops in India

What to do if you are pulled over by a traffic policeman:

1. **Pull Over Safely**: Slow down and stop at the side of the road.
2. **Identify the Officer**: A traffic police officer must be in uniform with a name tag. If they are in civil clothes, ask for their ID.
3. **Documents**: You must carry:
    - Driving License (DL)
    - Registration Certificate (RC)
    - Pollution Under Control (PUC) Certificate
    - Insurance Paper
    - Note: Digital copies on mParivahan or DigiLocker are valid.
4. **On-the-spot Fines**: Only an officer of the rank of Sub-Inspector or above can collect on-the-spot fines. They must provide an official receipt.

**Red Flags**:
- An officer cannot take your car keys or force you out of the vehicle without a valid reason.
- Do NOT offer a bribe to escape a fine.
- If the officer is aggressive or asks for a bribe, note down their buckle number/name.
